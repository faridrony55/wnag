Encore Karaoke Lounge

Encore is seven days a week of karaoke for over-21 bar patrons only!

  • Open 3PM - 2AM every night
  • Monday - Thursday karaoke starts at 8PM
  • Friday karaoke starts at 5PM
  • Saturday & Sunday karaoke starts at 4PM
  • 365 days a year

We are conveniently located on California Street, in between Polk and Larkin.

Encore has a great history, having been in the neighborhood for many years.

We represent the diversity of San Francisco. Everyone is welcome and you'll find an eclectic mix of friendly people there no matter what night… all ages, races, and orientations.

You'll see high class, middle class, low class and people who don't have any class! As long as you play well with others, you're welcome. Heck, we don't even care if you sing… come in and listen!

Whether you're a diva, rock star, diva wannabe, or karaoke virgin… this is the place to be. The environment is friendly, laid-back and supportive. We're all there to have fun!

We're conveniently located in the heart of the Polk at California.

Easy walking distance from the Van Ness and Polk MUNI lines, not to mention the cable car practically stops in front of our door. Cabs are plentiful at any time. There is street parking and a few lots in the neighborhood.

We're also close to some great restaurants like Tuba Express,  Cordon Bleu,  Swan Oyster Depot,  Ti Piacera  and Nara Sushi.

Any night is a good night to drop in.

We can get hopping Monday through Sunday as people get that urge to sing and head on in to belt one out or croon away.

On slower nights, you'll get to sing more and practice some of those songs you want to try out later in front of a packed house.

Saturday & Sunday afternoons starting at 4pm is a perfect time to practice to your heart's content with a very supportive crowd of regulars.

We can be host to your next party or event.

Please call us at (415) 775 - 0442 at least 2 weeks in advance for large or private parties. For smaller parties, contact us at least 2 days to reserve tables.
